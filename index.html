Welcome to Bishopdale Pathfinders!

Where are we?

We're located in Christchurch, Canterbury, in the beautiful South Island of New Zealand.

Christchurch is New Zealand's second largest city. From Christchurch, you can find the sparkling Pacific Ocean, the majestic Southern Alps and an ancient volcanic peninsula all within driving distance. Canterbury is a true show of God's artistic love, and at Bishopdale Pathfinders we take pride in our ability to share His art with every generation.

What do we do?

We meet at Bishopdale Seventh-Day Adventist Church on specific Sabbath afternoons, these club days consist of many things. Some things we get up to on club days are: book work, camp planning, drill and marching, playing games, worships, team building, confidence building, and most importantly connecting with God and with each other.

We also go on camps and tramps throughout the year, with some camps and tramps for everyone and some for our older Pathfinders. These camps give us a chance to explore our beautiful surroundings we're so blessed to live close to here in New Zealand. They also serve as a fantastic chance to learn more practical things such as camping skills, navigational skills, search and rescue, first aid, and so many more. As well as all the learning done on camps, there's always time for fun! A lot of games are played, memories made, and connections to God grown ever-stronger.
